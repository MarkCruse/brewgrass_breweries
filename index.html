Zoom In and Out: Use the zoom controls on the map, typically located in the top-left or top-right corner, to zoom in and out. Zooming in provides more detailed information, while zooming out gives you a broader view of the map.

Pan: Click and drag the map to pan around. This allows you to explore different areas of the map that are not currently visible. Panning is especially useful when the map is zoomed in, and you want to navigate to a different location.

Hover over/Click on Markers: Hover over and click on a marker to get more information about that location. A popup may appear with details like the name, address, and description of the place.

Open Marker Clusters: The map contains cluster markers, which are colored circles with the number of breweries in that area. To explore a cluster, click on it. The cluster will expand, revealing individual markers within it. This allows you to see more specific locations that were initially grouped together.
